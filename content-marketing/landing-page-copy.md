## FAQ Section (Enhanced)

### What is BizBoard Pro?

BizBoard Pro is an AI-powered venture dashboard that helps solopreneurs, side hustlers, and serial builders capture ideas, organize ventures, track progress, and know what to build next — all in one place.

**TL;DR:** It's the missing layer between "I have 10 ideas" and "I know which one to work on this week."

---

### Who is BizBoard Pro for?

**Perfect for:**
- Solopreneurs juggling 3+ business ideas
- 9-to-5ers with active side hustles
- Serial entrepreneurs who start (and sometimes abandon) projects
- Anyone who's ever said "I have too many ideas and not enough focus"

**Not for:**
- Teams managing ONE big project (use Asana)
- People happy with their current system (if it ain't broke...)
- Enterprise companies (we're built for scrappy builders)

### Does BizBoard Pro replace Notion, Trello, or Asana?

**No — and that's by design.**

BizBoard Pro is the layer ABOVE your execution tools. It helps you decide what deserves attention first, then you push the right work into Notion, Trello, or Asana with one click.

**Think of it like this:**
- BizBoard = Your brain's CEO (strategy, priorities)
- Notion/Trello/Asana = Your brain's workers (execution, tasks)

You keep the tools you already use. We just help you use them on the right things.
