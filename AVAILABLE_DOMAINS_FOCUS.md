# FlipAndSift - Available & Expired Domains Focus

## 🎯 Focus: Ready-to-Register Domains

**From:** All domain types (auctions, pending, etc.)  
**To:** Only available and expired domains (immediately registerable)

---

## ✅ What This Means

### **Available Domains**
- ✅ Dropped and ready to register NOW
- ✅ No auction bidding
- ✅ No waiting for drop
- ✅ Register immediately at Namecheap/GoDaddy

### **Expired Domains**
- ✅ Recently expired (redemption period)
- ✅ May have existing backlinks
- ✅ Can be backordered or wait for drop
- ✅ Higher value potential
