## 🚨 Common Launch Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ **Perfectionism** — Launch at 80%, not 100%
2. ❌ **No tracking** — You can't improve what you don't measure
3. ❌ **Ignoring feedback** — Early users tell you what to fix
4. ❌ **Set and forget ads** — Check daily, optimize weekly
5. ❌ **No email list** — Build it from day 1
6. ❌ **Inconsistent posting** — Daily social media presence matters
7. ❌ **Complicated onboarding** — Reduce friction everywhere
8. ❌ **No testimonials** — Collect them aggressively early
9. ❌ **Weak pricing** — Don't undervalue your product
10. ❌ **No clear CTA** — Every page needs one action
