## 🎬 Content Creation Tips

### Visual Style Guide
- **Colors:** Calming blues/purples (match your brand)
- **Fonts:** Clean, readable (Helvetica, Inter, Poppins)
- **Imagery:** Real people (not stock photos), calm settings, avoid tech anxiety
- **Tone:** Warm, approachable, never condescending

### Video Best Practices
- **Length:** 15-30 seconds for TikTok/Reels, 60-90 sec for YouTube Shorts
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds must grab attention
- **Captions:** Always add text overlays (85% watch without sound)
- **CTA:** Clear next step at the end
