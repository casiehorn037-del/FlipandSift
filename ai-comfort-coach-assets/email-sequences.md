## 📧 Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Rules
- Keep under 50 characters  
- Use emojis sparingly (1 max)  
- Make it curiosity-driven or benefit-driven  
- Avoid spam words: FREE, BUY NOW, LIMITED TIME (unless true)  

### Body Copy Rules
- Write like a human, not a corporation  
- Short paragraphs (2-3 lines max)  
- One clear CTA per email  
- Use "you" more than "we"  
- End with a P.S. (highest-read part of emails)  

### Send Time Best Practices
- Welcome emails: Immediate  
- Educational emails: 10am-12pm, Tue-Thu  
- Promotional emails: 6-8pm, Wed-Thu  
- Re-engagement: Weekends (people reflect on goals)  

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate (target: 25%+)  
- Click rate (target: 8%+)  
- Conversion rate (target: 2-5% for paid)  
- Unsubscribe rate (keep under 0.5%)
