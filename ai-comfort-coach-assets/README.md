## 💡 Key Success Factors

### 1. **Consistency**
Post daily on social media. Follow the calendar. Don't skip days.

### 2. **Speed**
Launch fast. Don't wait for perfection. Iterate based on real data.

### 3. **Testing**
A/B test everything: headlines, images, pricing, CTAs. Data > opinions.

### 4. **Feedback**
Talk to your first 100 users. Ask what they love and hate. Adjust.

### 5. **Retention**
Getting users is hard. Keeping them is harder. Focus on onboarding and progress tracking.
